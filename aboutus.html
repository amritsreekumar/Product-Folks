About Us

[Know our story]

Product Folks is a community which brings the product people together and conduct various activities in order to share knowledge among product managers and product enthusiasts. This community helps product people to socialize with fellow PMs, share their thoughts and discuss problems and trends in product space.

Product Management has seen a growing demand around the world but is still less explored in Indian ecosystem. This is a joint mission to educate our engineers (and non-engineers) about Product Management and share knowledge and grow together.
